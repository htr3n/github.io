About

Huy Tran (aka htr3n) was PhD candidate then postdoc at Vienna University of Technology (2005-2009, 2009-2011) and a senior postdoc at both University of Vienna (2011-2017) in Austria. Before that, he was a lecturer at HCM City University of Technology, Vietnam (2002-2005).

Since 2005, he had taken part in several European and national R&D projects such as WWTF CONTAINER, EU FP7 INDENICA, EU FP7 COMPAS, and SemBiz. His research interests are including software architecture, distributed systems, SOAs, event-driven architectures, model-driven engineering, and application of formal methods. Huy has also involved in software development, especially with Java, PHP, JavaScript, Python, Go, and shell scripting.

Huy’s specialities:

  • Software architecture analysis and design
  • Service-Oriented Architectures and Process-Driven SOAs (BPMN, BPEL)
  • Model-Driven Software Development
  • Event-based systems
  • Domain-specific languages (DSLs)
  • Eclipse Modeling Framework, Xpand/Xtend, and Xtext
  • Web and RESTful services, Spring, Apache CXF, Service Component Architecture
  • Java, PHP, Python, JavaScript, Go, Shell Scripting
