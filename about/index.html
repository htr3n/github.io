About

htr3n (aka Huy Tran) is a software researcher and developer. He earned his bachelor degree in 2002 and was a lecturer at HCM City University of Technology, Vietnam until October 2005. He got a scholarship from Austrian Federal Ministry of Education, Science, and Research and earned his Ph.D. in December 2009. Huy started working as software researcher at Vienna University of Technology (2009-2011) and University of Vienna (2011-2017) in Austria.

Since starting his research in 2005, he had taken part in several European and national R&D projects such as WWTF CONTAINER, EU FP7 INDENICA, EU FP7 COMPAS, and SemBiz. His research interests are including software architecture, distributed systems, SOAs, event-driven architectures, model-driven engineering, and application of formal methods.

Huy has extensively involved in software development in Java, PHP, JavaScript, Python, Go, and shell scripting. Many of his research outcomes have been transferred into software development techniques. He developed a view-based modelling framework (VbMF) using Eclipse/EMF/Xtext/Xtend that can be used to design process-driven SOAs and automatically generate code and configurations in BPMN/BPEL/WSDL/XML/Java. VbMF was used in collaboration with some European industrial partners for modelling and generating a large-scale loan approval process-centric system.

Another view-based modelling techniques have been developed for designing and generating code and configurations for virtual service platforms running on top of Service-Component Architectures and relevant remote communication technologies such as JMS and SOAP/REST/HTTP. This modelling tool suite has been used to design and implement a large-scale platform integration solution that incorporating parts of cloud-based Ruby-on-Rails Yard Management Systems, .NET/C++ Warehousing Management Systems, and Java based Realtime Monitoring Systems from European industrial partners.
