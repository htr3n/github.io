Vsc Dark Themes

Jul 29, 2018 in DEV
dev visual-studio-code theme
1 min read

Default Dark+

  • The background looks quite pleasant but variables in light blue and method calls on light yellow make it a bit too bright.

Dark (Visual Studio)

https://marketplace.visualstudio.com/items?itemName=gerane.Theme-Capo-Dark

  • Variables: dark blue
  • Keywords: dark purple
  • Function/method calls: dark green
  • Double quotes strings: dark red
  • The background is light black, thus, looks smooth and goes easy on the eyes

https://marketplace.visualstudio.com/items?itemName=Zyst.Egoist-One

  • Quite similar to Capo Dark, except the background is brighter
  • Variables: red/grey
  • Keywords: dark orange/yellow
  • Function/method calls: dark blue
  • Strings: dark green
